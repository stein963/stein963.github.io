About Me

I'm a third-year Physics student at the University of Nairobi, studying Electrodynamics, Thermodynamics, Solid State Physics, and Electronics. But what drives me most is what happens outside the classroom.

I build quantitative systems — backtesting pipelines, signal engines, live data dashboards — that help make sense of complex financial data. I started because I was curious. I kept going because the data kept surprising me.

"I hope to one day write an algorithm that will bring me money when I'm asleep."

Every project I build is a step toward that. I work primarily in Python, pulling from APIs like CFTC, Yahoo Finance, SEC EDGAR, and FRED to build systems that actually run — not just simulations.
